Project Description

This project is a 2D animation series depicting the inspiring stories of famous figures from the Bible, such as Ruth, Samson, Miriam, and John. Created using Adobe Illustrator and After Effects, the animations are designed to be engaging and easy to understand for young children from kindergarten to elementary school. The project was commissioned by GKI Kebon Jati Church, aiming to introduce timeless Biblical values to the next generation through colorful visuals and captivating storytelling.

Project Date March , 2024
Role Illustration & Motion, 2D Animation
Client GKI Kebon Jati
