Putri Indonesia

Project Date 20 Mei 2023
Role Motion Graphic, 2D Animation
Client Indosiar
Visit Surya Citra Media

    Puteri Indonesia is a prestigious national beauty pageant in Indonesia, established in 1992 and organized by the Yayasan Puteri Indonesia (YPI). It serves as a platform to select representatives for various international beauty contests, including Miss International, Miss Supranational, Miss Charm, and Miss Cosmo. The pageant is traditionally held annually in March, coinciding with International Women's Day celebrations.

    The upcoming Puteri Indonesia 2025 marks the 28th edition of the pageant and is scheduled to be held on 2 May 2025 at the Plenary Hall, Jakarta International Convention Center. Harashta Haifa Zahra of West Java, the reigning Puteri Indonesia 2024, will crown her successor at the conclusion of the event.

    Akademi Sahur Indonesia, abbreviated as AKSI, is a reality and talent search show broadcast on Indosiar during the pre-dawn meal (sahur) throughout the month of Ramadan. AKSI is a combination of reality and variety show formats aimed at discovering new ustadz and ustadzah (Islamic preachers) who have unique characteristics in delivering their sermons.

    Akademi Sahur Indonesia, often simply referred to as AKSI, presents a different kind of show that emphasizes togetherness. When the Indonesian Ulema Council (MUI) reviewed various TV programs that strayed from the true meaning of Ramadan, Akademi Sahur Indonesia a talent search that also incorporates comedy did not appear on MUI’s list of problematic programs.
